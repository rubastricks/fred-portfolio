Project B

2019 - Present

Martial Arts Tricking, commonly known as “Tricking” is a training discipline that originated in the late 1990s.
Tricking combines elements from Wushu, Taekwondo, Gymnastics and various extreme sports. The goal of Tricking
is not necessarily to engage in combat but to showcase skill, athleticism, and artistic expression. It involves
performing acrobatic flips, twists, and kicks, often stringing them together in a fluid and creative manner.

Project B provides a generational look at the Tricking community in the Greater Boston area and the experiences of
those who shape it.
